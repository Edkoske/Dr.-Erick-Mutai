About

His Excellency Dr. Erick Mutai leads Kericho County with a focus on pragmatic solutions, community partnership, and measurable impact across key sectors. With over two decades of experience in public service and development, Dr. Mutai has demonstrated unwavering commitment to transparent governance, citizen accountability, and inclusive economic growth that benefits all residents of Kericho.

Leadership Record

Targeted investments in agriculture, health, roads and skills training have driven greater access to markets and services for households across the county. Under his leadership, Kericho has seen improved infrastructure, enhanced primary healthcare services reaching 12 wards, expanded irrigation systems benefiting smallholder farmers, and strengthened technical training programs creating pathways to employment for youth. These tangible achievements reflect a commitment to sustainable development and prosperity for all.

Vision 2027–2032

A forward-looking strategy to expand opportunity, strengthen public services and ensure transparent, citizen-focused governance. The vision centers on agricultural modernization to boost rural incomes, universal healthcare access to all wards, completed road networks connecting all sub-counties, and youth skills programs creating 5,000+ jobs annually. Success requires partnership with communities, private sector, and development partners united in delivering results for Kericho's future.
