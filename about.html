About Dr. Erick Mutai

A results-oriented public servant committed to inclusive development, transparent governance and community-driven progress.

Under Dr. Mutai's leadership, the county has prioritized projects that strengthen the tea value chain, expand primary healthcare, improve roads and create opportunities for young people and small businesses.

Governor — Kericho County
Advocate — Farmers & Cooperatives

Leadership Journey & Credentials

Dr. Mutai's professional journey combines public service, policy work and partnership-building with civil society and the private sector to deliver measurable results for communities.

Academic & Professional Background

  • Advanced academic training and applied research experience in public policy and development
  • Extensive experience in county governance, programme delivery and stakeholder engagement
  • Proven track record of building public-private partnerships to scale impact

Core Values

Service, accountability, unity and inclusive development — the principles that guide policy priorities and programme delivery.
