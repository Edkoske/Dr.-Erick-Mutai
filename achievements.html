Leadership Record & Achievements

Agriculture & Tea Value Chain

  • Expanded irrigation and farmer training programmes that have strengthened productivity and market readiness for smallholders
  • Ongoing support for cooperatives and initiatives to increase value capture within the tea value chain

Healthcare

  • Upgraded and staffed primary healthcare facilities to improve access and service quality
  • Expanded community health outreach and targeted maternal and child health programmes

Infrastructure & Roads

  • Targeted road rehabilitation projects to improve connectivity between farms and markets, reducing transport costs and travel times

Education & Youth Empowerment

  • Comprehensive skills training, scholarships and youth enterprise support to expand livelihoods and entrepreneurship

Governance & Service Delivery

  • Improved public service delivery through digitized portals, transparency measures and regular public reporting
