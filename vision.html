Vision 2027–2032

Policy Pillars

  • Economic empowerment & job creation — boost small and medium enterprises, promote value-addition and expand market-based opportunities for youth.
  • Sustainable agriculture & investment — modernize the tea value chain, expand irrigation and improve farmer access to finance and markets.
  • Social services & inclusive growth — strengthen primary healthcare, improve learning outcomes, and expand social protection for vulnerable households.
  • Transparent & accountable governance — implement open-data practices, strengthen oversight and increase citizen engagement in decision-making.
